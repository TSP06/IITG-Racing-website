Formula Student Series

With experience in hand by manufacturing Origin, we started manufacturing this car in 2015 and completed it in the end of 2016 just in time to participate in SUPRA 2016. We also took this car to Formula Bharat 2017 where it ranked 21st. It is named after ‘Tachyon’, hypothetical particles that travel faster than light. This car clocked speeds up to 120 kmph powered by a Honda CBR 600cc engine with max torque of 66Nm. This car reached 0 to 60 kmph in 5-6 sec. Fabricated using fiber reinforced plastic and its Hoosier tires made it an absolute beast on the track. This car was also showcased in Techevince

We are a group of dedicated students, who design and fabricate Formula student race car prototypes
