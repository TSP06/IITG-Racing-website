Formula Student Series

The second formula style car, its manufacturing started in 2018. We had in hand experiences from manufacturing Tachyon 1.0 which helped us in making this car smoothly. This car participated in Formula Bharat 2020, unfortunately due to logistics issue this car couldn’t reach on time to the competition.This time we decided to work with a smaller engine, KTM RC 390cc which powered the car to achieve a maximum speed of 80 kmph. The body of this car was of Grey color made up of Fiber reinforced plastic. The tires used in this car were Hoosier 10.0x6.0 – 18.
